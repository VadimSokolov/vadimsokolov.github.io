QC Rules

IDRule
RULE-001 Expired documents MUST FAIL ‚Äî no exceptions for 'archival' or 'historical'
RULE-002 Documents >3 years old with no newer version MUST FAIL
RULE-003 Signatures reported as present MUST be visually confirmed in the page image ‚Äî text extraction does NOT preserve signatures
RULE-004 Never fabricate document content that isn't visible in the PDF
RULE-005 Always cite specific page numbers for findings
RULE-006 Distinguish between 'not present' and 'not visible/legible'
RULE-007 Report confidence accurately ‚Äî uncertainty is acceptable
RULE-008 For SIGNATURES: any mark in or near the signature area (handwritten, scribbled, stamped, digital, or partial) counts as a signature present ‚Äî PASS unless the signature area is completely blank with zero marks
RULE-009 For CROSS_REFERENCE, only FAIL if the person is explicitly prohibited from the site ‚Äî an unknown person who is not yet on the staff list should be flagged as an observation, not a FAIL
