School Programs

Chess Enrichment Programs

Comprehensive enrichment programs that build self-esteem and inspire academic excellence. Classes for Grades K-8.

üè´ Chess-Ed School Programs

Each week the students will enjoy an exciting hour of rich specialized chess instruction, with a professional instructor from Chess-Ed, plus time to play against other students and test their own understanding of the lesson of the day.

Beginners will develop basic skills required to enjoy the game, while continuing students will continue to grow in the knowledge of opening theory, middle game tactics and endgame combinations. Each student's vocabulary will be expanded as they continue to learn definitions for different tactics and strategies.

Our form of instruction takes each student to a new level of expertise and enjoyment. Don't miss out on this enrichment program which provides an intellectual foundation for all learning. CHESS--"the sport of the mind".
