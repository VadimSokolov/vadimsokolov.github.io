Introduces predictive analytics with applications in engineering, business, finance,health care, and social economic areas. Topics include time series and cross-sectional data processing, data visualization, correlation, linear and multiple regressions, classification and clustering, time series decomposition, factor models and causal models, predictive modeling performance analysis, and case study. Provides a foundation of basic theory and methodology with applied examples to analyze large engineering, social, and econometric data for predictive decision making. Hands-on experiments with R will be emphasized. \ Syllabus\
