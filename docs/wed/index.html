Wedding Day

Things not to be surprised on the wedding day:

  • There is valet parking at both Venues: $10 at the ceremony location and $8 at the banquet hall

  • There will be no food at the ceremony (Champaign and traditional Russian bread will be served). Please save your appetite for reception, there will be a ton of yummy food.

  • Ceremony will be held in English

  • We will sing a song during the ceremony (“Мы желаем счастья вам”), you can see the lyrics below

  • Reception will mostly be held in Russian, with some parts being bilingual

  • There will be a time gap between the ceremony and the reception (2:30-6pm). It is a nice opportunity to rest before partying all night or do some sightseeing. Venice beach, Santa Monica are in close proximity to the ceremony place

  • No transportation will be provided from the ceremony to the reception. It is a $20-$40 (35-40 min) Uber ride

  • 6-7pm is the cocktail hour with Hors d’oeuvres, and dinner starts at 7pm

  • Bring dollar bills to participate in our party games

  • Please use hashtag #sashadavidlove for social media photos and posts

  • If you would like to share your photos with us, please click here to upload your photos

  • Plan to party till 2am as music will not stop until then

  • Be ready for some crazy fun at our Russian/Armenian party!!!!!
